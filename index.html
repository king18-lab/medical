Diagnostic Excellence

Comprehensive Lab Tests

Accurate, reliable, and fast diagnostic services to support your health journey.

Complete Blood Count

Measures red & white blood cells, hemoglobin, platelets – essential for detecting infections, anemia, and overall health.

⏱️ Results in 4–6 hrs

Lipid Profile

Cholesterol, triglycerides, HDL, LDL – crucial for heart health and risk assessment.

⏱️ Results in 6–8 hrs

Thyroid Panel

T3, T4, TSH – essential for detecting thyroid disorders, metabolism, and energy levels.

⏱️ Results in 5–7 hrs

Diabetes Screening

Fasting glucose, HbA1c, oral glucose tolerance – monitor and manage blood sugar levels.

⏱️ Results in 4–6 hrs

Liver Function Test

ALT, AST, bilirubin, alkaline phosphatase – evaluate liver health and detect damage.

⏱️ Results in 6–8 hrs

Kidney Function Test

Creatinine, urea, uric acid – essential for monitoring kidney performance and filtration.

⏱️ Results in 5–7 hrs
